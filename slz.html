SU LI-ZHEN



The character Su Li-zhen, portrayed by Maggie Cheung, is depicted as a reserved and elegant woman. She is the epitome of grace and sophistication, trapped in a complex web of unfulfilled desires and societal constraints. As the female lead, Su Li-zhen occupies a unique position, navigating the delicate balance between her own yearning for love and her societal obligations as a married woman. Her character is both enigmatic and empathetic, embodying the quiet strength of someone trapped in a clandestine and unspoken love affair.
