CHOW MO-WAN



The character Chow Mo-wan, played by Tony Leung, is portrayed as a reserved and introspective individual. He exudes an air of quiet dignity and restraint, concealing his emotions beneath a veneer of composure. As the male lead, Chow Mo-wan occupies a complex position, finding himself entangled in a secretive and unspoken love affair while grappling with societal expectations and the constraints of his own heart. His character is a compelling blend of melancholy and longing, epitomizing the poignancy of unfulfilled desires and the enigmatic nature of forbidden love.
